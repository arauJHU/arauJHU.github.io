I am a researcher working in computational mechanics and materials modeling, with a focus on coupled chemo-mechanical processes in heterogeneous solids and extreme environments.

---

## Research Interests

My work focuses on understanding how mechanics, diffusion, and chemistry interact in complex materials systems.

- Chemomechanical coupling in solids  
- Continuum modeling of fracture and damage  
- Multiscale methods for heterogeneous materials  
- Phase-field and variational formulations of material failure  

---

## Current Projects

### Modeling fracture in reactive environments
Developing continuum frameworks for crack propagation in materials undergoing oxidation and diffusion-driven stress evolution.

### Multiscale chemo-mechanics
Bridging microscale diffusion processes with macroscale mechanical response in polycrystalline materials.
